About Us

CardioChem by Swastik Life Sciences

CardioChem, founded as a pioneering division of Swastik Life Sciences, is dedicated exclusively to advancing cardiac health.

Established to address the unique challenges of heart-related diseases, CardioChem innovates, manufactures, and supplies the highest quality cardiovascular medicines across India. Our operations are centrally managed from Chandigarh, and our network now extends through trusted franchise partners in more than 25+ states.

We created CardioChem by keeping customers in our mind. We made sure that the products we are supplying, products we are formulating are standard and follow every norm laid by national and international organization.
